# Snap Archive Viewer

Browse your Snapchat memories, chats, and history — completely offline, in your browser.

## What It Does

- **Memories** — View all your saved photos and videos, with overlay toggle for text/drawings
- **Chats** — Read through your full conversation history
- **Snap History** — See every snap sent and received
- **Search History** — Review past searches
- **Profile** — View account details and change history
- **Friends** — Browse your friends list

## Getting Your Data

Before using the app, download your Snapchat data:

### Step 1: Go to Settings > My Data
Open Snapchat → tap your profile icon → ⚙️ Settings → scroll to **My Data**

![Snapchat settings]([http://url/to/img.png](https://github.com/anthonyg102099/boohive/blob/main/1.png)

### Step 2: Select What to Download
Choose the data you want to view:
- Memories / Memories Media
- Chat History / Chat History Media
- Snap History
- Search History
- Account / Account History
- Friends

> **Tip:** Select all for the full experience!

### Step 3: Choose Date Range
Tap Next → select **All Time** for everything

### Step 4: Export & Wait
Tap Export Request → wait for Snapchat to prepare your data (can take minutes to hours) → download the .zip file
Extract all .zip files into a single parent folder (call it whatever you'd like)

## Controls

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Browse photos** | Scroll through memories |
| **View full screen** | Click a photo |
| **Next/Previous** | Arrow keys, ← → buttons, or swipe (touch) |
| **Toggle overlay** | Click ✨ button or press O |
| **Close viewer** | Click X or press Esc |
| **Search memories** | Type in the search box |
| **Filter** | Tap filter pills (Photos, Videos, Year) |

## Troubleshooting

**Images won't load:**
- Use Firefox instead of Chrome

**Page runs slow:**
- The app progressively loads more as you scroll
- Scroll slowly for best performance

**Need to reload fresh data:**
- Delete the app's cached data in browser settings, or
- Use incognito/private mode

## Privacy

- **Your data stays on your device** — nothing is uploaded anywhere
- **No tracking or analytics**
